Giselle Vargas

Artist/Studio Art Major

Hello, I am Giselle Vargas! Currently, I am a first-year university student majoring in Studio Art. I am not just your average student; I am a first-gen college student with an excellent student background. Coming from a low socioeconomic background, I have always been inspired to find the best in tough situations and strive for success. I am the oldest of 3 siblings, and I make it a responsibility to be a great role model. After all of my efforts, I can say that the many people in my life (family, friends, and mentors) are proud of me and continue to support me in my journey ahead.

I want to say that I've always been an artist. Ever since I can remember, I have always liked to draw and color. Even if it is considered a basic activity for children, that right there was the early marks of what I wanted to do in the future, well not exactly. At first I wanted to be a fashion designer, and then a chemist (crazy right). It wasn't until 7th grade where I really was starting to reflect on my future. I have always been someone who had many interest and can do well in various fields, but I didn't really know what I wanted to do. After pondering, I realized I needed to flip the question; what was it that I wanted to accomplish in my life. In all honesty, I really wanted to move people's hearts. I wanted to be someone that can share emotions, memories, and experiences with others and connect the world better. Yeah you can do that with literature or music, but I found that art is something that fits my style more. Specifically, I gravitated more towards animation. Animation is a form of art where people can look and see what the message is. You have multiple artists working together from various fields (writers, artists, composers, filmmakers, etc.) to create a project for others to enjoy and take away something. I really want to achieve that feeling of knowing someone carried what many wanted to invoke in their work. And so, ever since then, my dream is to become an animator!

As an artist, I have a good expertise in my field. My medium includes paper, pen, colored pencil, watercolor, and acrylic paint. I am not so acquainted with digital art since I was never able to get access to any material growing up. Though, something I do like to share is that I know some basics to 3D Animation. I took a free course after school during my junior year in high school to get myself better acquainted with it. That class had to be one of the best I ever took and I enjoyed ever moment of it, and it made me love animation more. Other than that, I have also designed and painted a school mural and participated in a few art contest. As an artist, you just have to trust the process!
